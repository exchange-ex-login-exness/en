Exness Login- Getting Started

To get into your trading environment, all you need to do is:

    1. Go to the official Exness website
    2. Press the Sign In button
    3. Type in your email and password
    4. Click Continue
    5. If you've enabled 2FA, enter the code

Once you are signed in, you will be sent to your Personal Area where all of the trading / account tools are found.
You will always want to be logging into the official website, to secure your login details.

Troubleshooting

If you have issues logging in still, take a look at the following options:

  • Forgot Password: You can use the option of "Forgot Password" and the system will give you instructions to change your login details.

  • Login Error: Check that you're logging into the correct email associated with your account.

  • 2FA Not Working: You should resync your authentication app or if you have a backup method to prove identity you may try it.

  • Page Not Loading: You might try clearing your browser cache or try logging in on another device.

If you are still experiencing problems, it is easy to reach Exness support, which is always available to give you help.
